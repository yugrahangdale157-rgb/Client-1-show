Who We Are

Two decades of measuring the ground truth.

Shri Associate is a land survey and architectural planning practice built on field accuracy and regulatory expertise.

Our Story

Built on the plot, not the brochure.

Shri Associate began as a small survey practice and has grown into a full-service land survey and architectural planning firm, serving landowners, developers, and government bodies.

Every project starts on-site. Our surveyors walk the boundary, verify records against physical markers, and only then move to documentation — because a plan is only as good as the data behind it.

Today our team handles land surveys, layout design, architectural planning and NA (Non-Agricultural) land conversion documentation under one roof.

Mission

To deliver land survey and architectural documentation that landowners, architects and authorities can rely on without question — accurate, timely, and fully compliant.

Vision

To be the most trusted survey and planning partner in the region, known for field precision and dependable turnaround on every engagement.
